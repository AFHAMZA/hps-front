Founder's Vision

AHMED HAMZA

Founder & Chief Innovation Officer

"Ahmed Hamza is an aerospace engineer, former military R&D officer, and the visionary founder of HPS Aviation. With over a decade of experience in missile mechanics and propulsion systems at the Egyptian Armed Forces Research Center, Ahmed specialized in developing high-performance propulsion technologies under extreme precision and operational demands."

In 2014, inspired by the flight of hummingbirds, he began exploring a new concept for aerial propulsion — one that would break the limitations of current eVTOL technologies. After years of intensive research, prototyping, and simulation using tools like MATLAB, ANSYS, and CFD, he created the Humming Propulsion System (HPS) — a compact, efficient, and tilt-free propulsion technology capable of revolutionizing how we fly.

Today, Ahmed leads HPS Aviation from Abu Dhabi Global Market, building not only the propulsion system of the future, but also its first product: CROW, a hydrogen-powered flying car that makes the dream of true personal air mobility a reality.
